Problem Statement

Traditional 3D virtual spaces are passive and disconnected — users can explore, but cannot ask questions, mark assets, or get intelligent guidance. Facilities managers spend hours manually cataloguing physical assets from floor plans, while building occupants have no natural-language way to navigate.

3DAgent bridges immersive 3D environments with large language models and computer vision, transforming static digital twins into interactive AI-powered agents.

Key Features
🗣️
Natural Language Navigation
Say "I want to cook" — 3DAgent maps intent to location and teleports you instantly using Matterport sweep commands.
📷
AI Vision Asset Detection
Screenshot any viewport and HuggingFace transformer models detect, classify, and auto-tag visible objects to your inventory.
🗺️
Interactive Floor Plan Minimap
Real-time canvas minimap tracks your position and overlays asset markers. Export annotated floor plans as PNG or PDF.
🎤
Voice Input Control
Hands-free exploration via Web Speech API. Speak commands to navigate, tag locations, or query your asset inventory.
📦
Smart Asset Inventory
Full CRUD asset management with category organisation, scan history, and one-click CSV/PDF export for facilities teams.
⚡
Dual-Model AI on Groq
Llama 4 Scout 17B handles all vision tasks (object detection, room naming), while LLaMA 3.3 70B powers text intent routing and chat — both served via Groq for sub-second latency.

Key Innovations
01

Digital Twin + LLM Fusion

First-of-its-kind integration of Matterport 3D digital twins with a 70B-parameter large language model for real-time spatial intent understanding and autonomous navigation.

02

Multimodal Input Pipeline

Seamlessly combines text chat, voice commands, and visual screenshots into a unified AI pipeline — enabling fully hands-free space exploration and cataloguing.

03

Activity-Driven Spatial Reasoning

Maps high-level human activities ("I want to sleep", "need to exercise") to physical locations using LLM reasoning rather than keyword matching — making navigation truly intelligent.

04

Vision-Grounded Asset Management

Llama 4 Scout 17B detects and counts objects from viewport screenshots via Groq, grounded by spatial metadata (sweep position, floor level). BLIP VQA provides an offline fallback — ensuring the system works with or without cloud connectivity.

Conclusion

Bridging Physical Spaces with Artificial Intelligence

3DAgent demonstrates that large language models and computer vision can be seamlessly integrated with immersive 3D digital twins to unlock entirely new modes of spatial interaction. By combining Llama 4 Scout 17B for vision-grounded asset detection, LLaMA 3.3 70B for natural language intent routing, and the Matterport SDK for real-time 3D navigation, the system transforms passive virtual environments into intelligent, conversational agents.

The result is a platform where users can speak, scan, and navigate — replacing manual facility audits and static floor plans with an AI-driven pipeline that is fast, scalable, and accessible to non-technical users.
